MEET LIANA


Welcome!! My name is Liana! Born and raised just outside of Boston, I received a BFA in Musical Theatre at the Hartt School in Connecticut with a minor in Multimedia Web Design and Development (fueled by many matcha lattes). While I’ve stayed close to my native New England roots, I have an affinity for traveling, exploring new places, and meeting new people. When I’m not performing, I spend my time exploring my other artistic passion, photography. Grab a seat, stay a while, and click around to get to know me!
