Photography and Me

I've had a long journey with photo. My mother and sister are both visual artists so I grew up in a very artistic household. I didn't quite inherit the drawing and painting skillset from my mother, so I had found my artistic expression in theatre. My sister is 7 years older than me, so when she was in high school, she always used me for her photography projects. Eventually, she went off to college at Cooper Union to continute to study art (and funnily enough, she ended up working in web development at Fathom in Boston post grad). Once I was in high school, I ended up studying photo like my sister, and I loved it. I took Photo all 4 years of high school, working with a mix of digital and film. Once I got to college, I didn't find myself taking photos anymore, that is until the end of my sophomore year. I went to a cafe with a couple of friends who had expressed interest in doing a photoshoot for fun. It was then that I remembered how much I enjoyed photo, and I started to offer my services to my classmates.

Below are a mix of photos from branding shoots, to grad photos, to landscape. Enjoy!
